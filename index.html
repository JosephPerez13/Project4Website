Making Food Taste Better

Established in 1960. Chuck's Hamburgers has been adding to the local flavor of Stockton, CA since 1960. We love doing what we do, which is to serve high quality, classic American fare and breakfast favorites in the heart of our community! Over the years, we've become a neighborhood landmark, and we look forward to serving our locals for many more years to come.
