About Us:

Chuck's Hamburgers been around since Chuck and Marie Grant first set up shop in 1960, and we've been Stockton's favorite family-run restaurant ever since! When you first step inside, you might first notice our old fashioned booths, or perhaps the antiques on the walls, or possibly our country window. We want to provide a cozy and comfortable environment for our community. Don't know what to order? If you ask our regulars, they might tell you to try our award-winning breakfast, including our "frisbee-sized" pancakes and crispy hash browns. Need a perk? Order some coffee and feel free to pour yourself a cup from behind our counter! A tradition in our neighborhood, we're honored to see second, third, and even fourth generations of our customers drop in and love us just as much as their parents and grandparents did, so drop by today and come see why we've been voted "Best Breakfast in Stockton"!
